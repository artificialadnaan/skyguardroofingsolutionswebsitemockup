Roof Condition Reports

Protect Your Investment with a Professional Inspection

Your roof is one of the most important components of your property, shielding everything inside from the elements. Yet most homeowners and business owners rarely think about their roof until a problem arises. A professional roof inspection from SkyGuard Roofing Solutions gives you the knowledge you need to make confident decisions about your property's future.

Our certified inspectors evaluate every critical aspect of your roofing system — from shingles and flashing to gutters, ventilation, and the underlying decking. We check for storm damage, manufacturer defects, improper installation, and normal wear and tear that can lead to costly repairs if left unaddressed.

Best of all, our 25-point roof inspection is completely free for qualified DFW homeowners and commercial property owners. There's no obligation — just honest, transparent information about the current state of your roof. Whether you're planning a sale, filing an insurance claim, or simply want peace of mind, our inspection report gives you the full picture.

Our 6-Step Inspection Process

1

Initial Assessment

Visual inspection of your roof's exterior and interior for signs of wear, damage, or aging.

2

Manufacturer Defects

Examination of roofing materials for signs of manufacturing defects or premature failure.

3

Storm Damage

Thorough assessment for hail, wind, and heavy rain damage that may not be visible from the ground.

4

Installation Evaluation

Attic inspection for insulation adequacy, ventilation, and energy efficiency concerns.

5

Detailed Recommendations

Comprehensive report with repair or replacement options tailored to your specific situation.

6

Transparent Communication

Our team is always available to answer questions and guide you through next steps.
