Our Story

Roofing Is Our Family Legacy

At SkyGuard Roofing Solutions, roofing isn't just our profession — it's our family legacy. As a second-generation roofer serving Dallas-Fort Worth with over 15 years of roofing experience and more than 30 years in construction, we bring unmatched knowledge, skill, and dedication to every project we take on.

As a family-owned and operated roofing contractor in DFW, we've built our reputation on treating every customer like family. Our foundation rests on integrity, ethics, and a strong moral compass, ensuring that every job is completed with honesty and excellence.

Our Expertise

Insurance Claims & Industry Knowledge

Navigating insurance claims can be overwhelming. Our team is fluent in Xactimate — the industry-standard estimating software used by insurance adjusters — so we speak their language and ensure accurate, fair settlements for every claim.

We've built strong relationships with top material suppliers and stay current with the latest roofing technologies, building codes, and best practices. When storms hit DFW, we're ready with emergency response and expert damage assessment.
